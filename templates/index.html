How it works Our goals

Why Coke Impact Rewards?

One program, two ways to earn, four clear goals—for you and for the planet.

  • Increase Coca‑Cola product sales — QR loyalty and bundle bonuses make every purchase rewarding.
  • Encourage brand loyalty — Points, Impact Credits, and exclusive merchandise keep fans coming back.
  • Reduce environmental waste — AI-powered recycling rewards only Coke cans and bottles, turning trash into impact.
  • Strengthen Coca‑Cola’s sustainability branding — A single, credible program that links purchase and recycle to real rewards.

Reward for Purchase

A QR-based loyalty system: every product has a unique code that activates only after you pay. Scan in the app, earn points, redeem for exclusive merchandise.

📱

Unique QR on every product

Every Coca‑Cola product includes a unique QR code. After purchase, the code is linked to POS verification—so it only activates once the sale is complete.

✅

Activation after purchase

QR codes activate only after purchase, linked to point-of-sale (POS) verification. No scanning in-store without buying—this prevents fraud and ensures rewards go to real customers.

📲

Scan in the app

Customers scan the code in the Coke Impact Rewards app to earn points. Simple, fast, and tied to their account for secure redemption.

📦

Bundle bonus

Bundle purchases unlock bonus points through a special bundle scan at checkout. More products, more points—driving larger basket sizes and repeat visits.

✨

Influencers & social challenges

Influencer sponsorships and social media challenges promote scanning and brand engagement. Limited-time codes and challenges keep the program top of mind.

Fraud prevention & repeat purchases

How we prevent fraud

QR codes are inactive until the sale is confirmed at the register. One code, one redemption. No in-store scanning without payment, no sharing codes from unpurchased products.

How we drive repeat purchases

Points add up toward exclusive merchandise (hats, shirts, hoodies, socks). Bundle scans offer extra points, and seasonal challenges give reasons to buy and scan again—building habit and loyalty.

Reward for Recycling

An AI-powered recycling system: deposit only Coca‑Cola cans and bottles. AI verifies product, material, weight, and quantity—then issues Impact Credits and shows your environmental impact.

10 points per can or bottle — every recycled Coke can or bottle earns you 10 points toward rewards.

🥤

Coca‑Cola only

Users deposit only Coca‑Cola cans and bottles. AI verifies that each item is an authentic Coca‑Cola product before any reward is issued.

🤖

AI verification

AI identifies material type (aluminum, plastic, etc.), weight, and quantity recycled. Total environmental impact is calculated from real data—not estimates.

🌱

Carbon saved

The system calculates total impact and shows users how much carbon they’ve saved. Your recycling history turns into a clear, shareable environmental story.

💳

Impact Credits

Earn “Impact Credits” for every verified recycle. The system generates a QR code that converts Impact Credits into reward points—redeemable in the app and on this site.

📊

Lifetime recycling data

All recycling is tracked inside the app: total items, weight, material mix, and carbon saved over time. Clear stats keep you informed.

🔍

Special Scan Mode

Special Scan Mode detects how many items are recycled at once and confirms they’re Coke products. Fast drop-off, instant verification, instant Impact Credits.

Recycling Leaderboard

See who’s saving the planet. Top recyclers by region, country, and worldwide. Every can and bottle counts — 10 points each.

Privacy & security

We store recycling data by user ID (e.g. User_ID_48291 → recycled 14 bottles), not by your name. Personal info is stored separately and encrypted. If data is ever breached, your identity stays protected. You can turn on Private setting in the app so your name never appears on the leaderboard—you’ll show only as your user ID. Shipping info is deleted after your order is fulfilled for your security, unless you choose to keep it for faster checkout next time.

🔐 Data Privacy Protection

Since you scan QR codes and may create an account, we commit to:

  • Do not collect unnecessary personal data
  • Clearly explain what data is collected
  • Allow you to delete your account at any time
  • No selling of user data
